We will present our covid stories with a writing narrative, pictures, videos, word cloud, data visualizations, audios, etc.
We envision some parts of the web layout to be a juxtaposition of two columns, each demonstrating the different lifestyles we wish to show, while the other parts being a cohesive body of tex and images. 
We also plan to add a sticky side bar to navigate the readers through the digital story.
