Reflections

At the present moment, looking back at what has happened in the past year, we see how the world has changed from various aspects. As two college students who made different decisions upon the outbreak of the pandemic, we both faced challenges in our lives.Â 

Attending Zoom University, Judy had to cope with the time-zone difference, while Jessica also had to adjust to the online platform, attending lectures without being physically on campus. During the pandemic, our real-life interactions with people were limited; hence, we treasured the opportunity of meeting and spending time with our friends. At times when we were feeling pressured and full of negativity, our families and friends were the ones who brought a ray of sunshine in the darkness.Â 

As we returned to campus this fall quarter, everything felt so new yet so familiar. Coming through the pandemic, we have never cherished the time in campus more. We are grateful for the past journey, and are more than ready to welcome the new challenges.
